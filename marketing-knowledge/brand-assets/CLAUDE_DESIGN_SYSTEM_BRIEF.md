## WHO WE ARE

Uno Más Tacos & Tequila — a modern Mexican restaurant and tequila bar in Spokane, WA. Three distinct venues at one address: The Cantina (main floor, converted mechanic's garage), The Mezzanine (upstairs speakeasy and private event space), and The Patio (outdoor bar + street food kitchen). Founded 2022. Located at 2020 N Monroe St, Spokane WA 99201.

**Tagline:** Get a little lost.
**Tone:** Confident, playful, self-aware. Short sentences. Never corporate.

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Uno Más
- **Headlines / Display:** Antonio (Bold, All Caps for impact headlines)
- **Body / Subheads:** Montserrat (Regular, Medium, SemiBold)
- **Fallback system fonts:** Georgia (serif) or system-ui (sans-serif)

### The Mezzanine
- **Titles:** DIN Condensed VF
- **Body:** Poppins
- **Accents / Decorative:** Baka Too

---

## DESIGN VOICE RULES

- Short sentences. Fragments welcome.
- Never use: "mouthwatering," "culinary journey," "artisanal," "mixology," "vibrant," "amazing," "perfect for any occasion"
- DO use: "House-smoked," "craft cocktails," "Get a little lost," "Modern Mexican"
- Price confidence — never apologize for or explain pricing
- The Mezzanine is always "cool older sibling" energy — minimal, moody, 0–1 emojis
- Lunch content: casual, energetic, 3–5 emojis
- Dinner content: confident, considered, 1–2 emojis max, always include reservation CTA

## KEY COPY BLOCKS

**Primary One-Liner (Long-form)**
> Uno Más is a modern Mexican restaurant and tequila bar in Spokane where the food is serious, the atmosphere is alive, and the only thing we take lightly is ourselves.

**Short Form (Social / Ads)**
> Modern Mexican. Craft cocktails. A speakeasy upstairs. The food is serious. The vibe is not. Get a little lost.

**Atmosphere**
> Inside. Feels like outside. Feels like somewhere else. Modern Mexican and tequila bar at 2020 N Monroe.

**Dinner / Occasion**
> The kind of dinner you'll still be talking about Sunday. Modern Mexican, craft cocktails, a speakeasy upstairs. Get a little lost.

### 🛍️ RETAIL / MERCH DESIGNS

**For T-Shirts:**
```
Design a high-fidelity t-shirt mockup for Uno Más Tacos & Tequila. 
Brand colors: Hot Pink #E22690, Electric Blue #18BCDC, Navy #003366, Yellow #FFEC00. 
Typography: Antonio for headlines (bold, condensed), Montserrat for supporting text.
Design style: Bold, graphic, street-wear influenced — not cheesy restaurant merch. 
Should feel like something people actually want to wear. 
Include the tagline "Get a little lost." on the back or sleeve.
Front should lead with logo or a strong graphic element, not text-heavy.
Show front and back views on a flat lay or lifestyle mockup.
```

**For Hats:**
```
Design a hat embroidery mockup for Uno Más Tacos & Tequila.
Colors: Hot Pink #E22690 on black or navy base. Antonio font.
Two options: (1) wordmark "Uno Más" centered front panel, (2) logo icon or tagline "Get a little lost." 
Clean, minimal — looks like a hat you'd buy at a boutique, not a restaurant freebie.
Show on a structured 6-panel snapback or dad hat profile.
```

**For Stickers:**
```
Design a sticker sheet for Uno Más Tacos & Tequila.
Include 4–6 sticker designs: (1) wordmark logo, (2) "Get a little lost." script, 
(3) a bold graphic element (taco, margarita glass, or abstract brand shape), 
(4) "2020 N Monroe" location sticker, (5) The Mezzanine sub-brand sticker (use #E22790 / #93009B palette on black).
Style: Die-cut, high contrast, works on laptops/water bottles/car bumpers.
Brand colors: #E22690, #18BCDC, #003366, #FFEC00.
```

## HOW TO USE THIS IN CLAUDE DESIGN

1. **Start a new project** in Claude Design → select "High fidelity" → "Prototype" or "Other"
2. **In your first message**, paste the relevant prompt from this doc + "Use the Uno Más design system above"
3. **Reference colors by hex** — Claude Design responds well to specific hex codes
4. **For Mezzanine projects** — always specify "use Mezzanine sub-brand, NOT main Uno Más palette"
5. **Iterate** — after first generation, ask for: "Darken the header," "Make the CTA button bigger," "Add the reservation link in the footer"
