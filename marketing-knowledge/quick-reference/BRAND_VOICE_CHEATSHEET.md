# Uno Más — Brand Voice Cheat Sheet
*The fast reference for anyone writing copy: humans or AI*

---

## The Short Version

**We are:** Bold. Warm. Local. Unpretentious. The friend who always knows where to eat.
**We are not:** Corporate. Cheesy. Hype-y. Generic.

---

## Personality in 5 Words

| Trait | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Confident** | We know our tacos are great. We don't need to over-explain. |
| **Warm** | We actually care about our customers and community. |
| **Witty** | Clever without trying too hard. Smart, not snarky. |
| **Real** | No fluff, no buzzwords, no corporate speak. |
| **Local** | Spokane isn't a backdrop — it's part of who we are. |

---

## Voice in Practice

### DO write like this:
- "Yeah, we put it on everything. Zero regrets."
- "Your Friday plans just got better."
- "Tacos are our love language."
- "Three locations. One obsession."
- "Bring the whole crew."
- "Made fresh. Eaten fast. No shame."

### DON'T write like this:
- "Experience the authentic flavors of Mexico..." ❌
- "We are committed to providing an exceptional dining experience." ❌
- "Indulge in our deliciously crafted offerings." ❌
- "Perfect for all your catering needs!" ❌
- "Visit us today at any of our convenient locations!" ❌

## Word Bank

### Words we use:
`crew` · `familia` · `más` · `good times` · `fresh` · `scratch-made` · `Spokane` · `regulars` · `real` · `bold` · `vibrant` · `always room for one more`

### Words we avoid:
`delicious` · `amazing` · `perfect` · `experience` · `authentic` · `indulge` · `crafted` · `exceptional` · `innovative` · `passionate about`

## Tone by Channel

| Channel | Tone Dial | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Instagram | Playful + punchy | Short, visual-first, one clear thought |
| TikTok / Reels | High energy, casual | First 2 seconds must hook — no slow builds |
| Facebook | Warm, community-forward | Slightly more copy OK, family/local vibe |
| Email | Personal, direct | Write like a real person, not a newsletter |
| SMS | Casual text from a friend | 160 chars, immediate value, no corporate |
| Google Ads | Clear + direct | Lead with the offer or location, no fluff |
| Review responses | Human, genuine | Reference their specific comment, brief |
| Catering / Events | Confident + easy-breezy | Make it sound like zero work for them |
| The Mezzanine | Slightly elevated | Still warm, but more polished than Uno Más |

## The Mezzanine — Sub-Brand Voice Note

The Mezzanine is the private event venue above the Wonder Building. It shares Uno Más DNA but has its own tone: a little more polished, more atmosphere-forward. Think: "the cool older sibling."

- Uno Más: "Bring the whole crew for tacos and tequila."
- The Mezzanine: "An unforgettable evening — with tacos and tequila."

The Mezzanine palette is electric pink/magenta + watercolor backgrounds. Keep copy elegant but never stuffy.

---

## Formatting Rules

- **Sentence case** for headlines (not Title Case Every Word)
- **Short sentences.** One idea per sentence when possible.
- **No exclamation marks** in every sentence — use sparingly for real emphasis
- **Emojis:** optional, never more than 2 per post, food/local/fun only
- **Hashtags:** end of Instagram posts only — never in the body copy
- **CTAs:** direct and simple — "Order now." "Tag a friend." "Reserve your table." Not "Click here to explore our offerings!"

---

## Quick Test

Before publishing anything, ask: *Would a real Uno Más regular say this out loud?*

If yes → publish.
If it sounds like a press release, a chain restaurant, or a marketing textbook → rewrite.
