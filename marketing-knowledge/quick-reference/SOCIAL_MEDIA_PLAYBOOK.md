## Content Pillars

### 1. Food & Drink (40%)
The core. Hero shots, close-ups, seasonal items, new menu drops, specials.
- Tacos in all forms — always a win
- Margarita pours, tequila shots, drink builds
- Fiesta packs + catering spreads
- Seasonal/limited items (Bloody Mary Bash, etc.)

### 2. Community & Culture (25%)
What makes Uno Más Spokane's modern Mexican restaurant and tequila bar, not just a restaurant.
- Regulars, shoutouts, fan reposts
- Local events and partnerships
- Spokane landmarks, local pride, city moments
- Staff + behind-the-scenes (humanizes the brand)

### 3. Events & Programming (20%)
Drive foot traffic and awareness for in-store events.
- Rotating specials, seasonal programming, Bloody Mary Bash
- The Mezzanine private events
- Holidays: Cinco de Mayo, Game Days, NYE
- ⚠️ Do NOT reference: Trivia Night, Comedy Night, Bingo — not currently active

### 4. Promotions & Offers (15%)
Conversion-focused content — never more than ~15% or it feels salesy.
- Rotating specials, loyalty rewards (Uno Mas Rewards: The Cantina Club)
- Catering promos, Fiesta Pack announcements
- Email/SMS signup pushes
- ⚠️ Do NOT reference happy hour or Taco Tuesday — not currently active

## Content Formats by Platform

### Instagram Feed
- **Square (1:1)** or **Portrait (4:5)** — portrait performs best in feed
- High-quality food photography or designed graphics
- Minimal text overlay — let the image breathe
- Caption: 1–3 sentences + CTA + line break + hashtags

### Instagram Reels / TikTok
- **Vertical (9:16)**, full screen
- Hook in first 2 seconds — motion, text, or bold visual
- Add captions/text overlays (many watch without sound)
- Trending audio when relevant
- Keep under 30 seconds for maximum completion rate

### Instagram Stories
- **Vertical (9:16)**
- Mix of: polls, questions, countdowns, behind-scenes, reposts, swipe-up links
- Don't overthink it — quick, candid, real
- Use location sticker (Spokane) for local reach

### Facebook
- **Landscape (16:9)** or square
- More context in copy than Instagram — Facebook readers actually read
- Tag other local businesses when relevant
- Event posts: use Facebook Events feature for programming

## Engagement Rules

1. **Reply to every comment within 24 hours** — especially questions and reviews
2. **Like and respond to DMs** — especially catering and Mezzanine inquiries
3. **Repost UGC** (user-generated content) to Stories — always credit the original poster
4. **Engage back** — like/comment on local Spokane food accounts and community pages
5. **Don't ignore negative comments** — address calmly, take it to DMs if needed

- Post a food photo on Tuesday — lunch special reminder performs well

- Repost at least 1 piece of UGC per week
