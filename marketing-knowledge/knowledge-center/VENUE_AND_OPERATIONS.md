## 4. THE MEZZANINE

### Physical Description
- **Location:** Upstairs — second floor of the Monroe flagship
- **Concept:** Speakeasy / tequila lounge — hidden, elevated, intentional
- **Capacity:** 28 guests (seated)
- **Features:** Dedicated bar, dumbwaiter for food service, fireplace, leather lounges, dramatic lighting
- **Access:** Via staircase from main floor
- **Feel:** Darker, moodier, more intimate than the main floor

### The Mezzanine Identity
The Mezzanine is Uno Más's upstairs secret. It has its own branding (separate logo, color palette: Electric Pink #E22790, Magenta #BF28BF, Ultra Violet #93009B, Charcoal, Black, White), its own fonts (DIN Condensed VF, Poppins, Baka Too), and its own atmosphere.

Content for the Mezzanine should feel elevated but not pretentious. The mystery is the marketing. You don't over-explain it — you let people discover it.

### Mezzanine Use Cases
- Private events (see MENU_AND_OFFERS.md — The Essentials, The Fiesta, The Full Send packages)
- Late-night atmosphere
- Date nights, special occasions
- Small group experiences

### Content Angles — The Mezzanine
- "There's a room upstairs." (Don't over-explain it)
- Fireplace + leather + cocktails that take their time
- The hidden speakeasy discovery feeling
- Private events — "your group deserves its own space"

Use Core Voice for Mezzanine content — slightly more elevated, fewer exclamation points, more scene-setting. The Party Promoter persona is appropriate for Mezzanine event announcements only.

## 7. THE BRAND STORY

### Origin
Uno Más was founded in 2022 by Ramsey Pruchnic, Chef Chad White, and Brewer Travis Thosath — emerging directly from the COVID-19 pandemic. The three converted the struggling Hello Sugar location in Spokane Valley into Uno Más: a bold taco concept with exceptional tequila drinks and a commitment to genuine hospitality.

### Evolution
Over two years in Spokane Valley, the concept was refined and the team built a loyal following. At the end of 2023, the decision was made to exit the Valley location and build a true flagship — a space worthy of the full Uno Más vision.

A lease was signed in January 2024 on North Monroe Street. Construction began using repurposed equipment from the Valley location. The Monroe flagship opened December 27, 2024.

### The Monroe Flagship
A two-story venue: main floor cantina (60+ seats) + The Mezzanine upstairs (28 seats) + seasonal back patio (40+ seats). Designed by contractor Tyler Stitt to feel like an indoor Mexican patio. Located in a rapidly growing district, five minutes north of downtown Spokane.

### Why No Delivery
*"We want to create more than just a meal — we want to create memories. We want to host you, to welcome you into our home, and to share with you the full Uno Más experience."* — Ramsey Pruchnic

### The Name
"Uno Más" — one more. It's an invitation and a state of mind. One more taco. One more round. One more reason to stay.
