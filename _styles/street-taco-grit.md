# Street Taco Grit

## Description

Textured, raw, poster-inspired taco shop style. Inspired by hand-stenciled menus, riso prints, and old taquería signage. Lower energy than Bold Cantina, but more visceral and food-forward. Tom Segura register — confident specificity, no try-hard.

## Hidden prompt

Create a textured, gritty design inspired by handmade taquería posters and risograph prints for Uno Más Tacos & Tequila. Use rough screenprint textures, limited 2-color palette from the Uno Más palette, raw-edged photography (often duotoned), and bold all-caps typography. Should feel like a flyer stapled to a telephone pole, not a corporate ad. House-smoked, scratch-made, Spokane.

## Visual keywords

textured, raw, hand-printed, riso, grungy, street, screenprint, taquería, poster, scratch-made

## Color notes

Two-color palette per asset (pick from Ramsey's main palette):

- Hot Pink `#E22690` + Navy `#003366`
- Yellow `#FFEC00` + Navy `#003366`
- White + Navy `#003366`
- Hot Pink `#E22690` + Black `#1A1A1A`

Avoid full-color photography — convert to duotone or high-contrast.

## Typography notes

All caps, condensed sans serif, slight grain or print misregistration. Antonio Bold (per design system) tracked tight. Acceptable: subtle letter overlap. Hand-stencil-style spacing variations.

## Layout notes

Asymmetric, info-dense, like a poster. Headline at the top stenciled large. Photo (often duotoned) in the middle. Body info as numbered or bulleted list. Strong horizontal rules between sections.

## Photography notes

Tight-cropped food shots, often duotoned. Hands in frame. Real grease/sauce visible. Imperfect plating preferred. NO clean studio shots. House-smoked carnitas, birria, single-item hero treatment.

## Example use cases

- Birria Tacos limited release
- Chef's Pick weekly drops
- Hot sauce / spicy menu items
- Late-night menu / after-hours promos
- Print posters for the venue
- T-shirt graphics
- Scratch-made / house-smoked storytelling

## When NOT to use this style

- Cocktail or premium drink promos → use Premium Cocktail Lounge
- Family-oriented announcements → use Family Fiesta
- Reservation/upscale dining → use Modern Mexican Minimal

## References

## Anti-references

- NOT polished — print misregistration is a feature
- NOT four-color — strict 2-color
- NOT corporate-stock photography
