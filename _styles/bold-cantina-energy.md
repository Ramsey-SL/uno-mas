# Bold Cantina Energy

## Description

The Uno Más default for parties, specials, and any campaign that needs to feel loud and celebratory. Bert Kreischer voice register — high energy, full personality, 3-5 emojis OK.

## Hidden prompt

> Internal-only direction injected into AI generations. Not shown to editors.

Create a bold cantina-inspired design for Uno Más Tacos & Tequila with high energy, vibrant contrast, playful typography, warm Mexican restaurant colors, lively food and drink focus, and a fun social-first layout. Lead with the experience. The vibe is a Friday night that turns into a story.

## Visual keywords

festive, bold, vibrant, playful, energetic, loud, high-contrast, party, weekend

## Color notes

Lean on the main Uno Más palette (NOT Mezzanine):

- Primary: Hot Pink `#E22690`
- Depth: Navy `#003366`
- Accent: Yellow `#FFEC00`
- Bright: Electric Blue `#18BCDC`
- Background: White `#FFFFFF`

Pink dominant. Yellow as energy. Navy for grounding. Avoid letting Navy dominate — Pink is hero.

## Typography notes

Per `marketing-knowledge/brand-assets/CLAUDE_DESIGN_SYSTEM_BRIEF.md`:
- Headlines: Antonio Bold (all caps OK for impact)
- Body: Montserrat (Regular/Medium/SemiBold)

Tight letter-spacing on display headlines. 1-2 weights total per asset.

## Layout notes

Large food or drink hero image dominant, energetic graphic overlays (geometric shapes, bold lines, Memphis-style triangles), strong CTA placement at bottom or right. Headline typically in a colored block.

## Photography notes

Top-down food shots, warm tungsten light, condensation on glass, raw textures (Mexican textile, dark wood). Per Ramsey's brand: "Inside feels like outside." Lean into the cantina-floor atmosphere.

## Example use cases

- Cinco de Mayo promotions
- Margarita specials / pitcher promos
- Weekend party announcements
- Live music / DJ nights
- Happy hour pushes (3-5pm daily)
- Patio season opening

## When NOT to use this style

- Premium/upscale tasting events → use Premium Cocktail Lounge
- Family/brunch promotions → use Family Fiesta
- Refined editorial/menu features → use Modern Mexican Minimal
- Late-night Mezzanine content → use Premium Cocktail Lounge

## References

- Punch-out white type on hot pink fields
- Bright top-down margarita photography (salt rim, lime wedge)

- NOT Mezzanine palette (`#E22790`, `#BF28BF`, `#93009B`) — those are sub-brand only
