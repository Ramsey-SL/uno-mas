# Channel Strategy — Dinner Hits Different Now

> Campaign: 2026-03-dinner-menu-revamp
> Total Budget: $2,500
> Date Range: March 28 → April 27, 2026
> Location: 2020 N Monroe St, Spokane, WA 99201

---

## Channel Breakdown

### Meta (Facebook + Instagram) — @unomastacoshop

**Role in campaign**: Primary paid channel — awareness + retargeting
**Budget**: $1,800 ($1,200 prospecting + $600 retargeting)
**Start date**: March 28 (full budget, day 1)

**Audience targeting**:
- Prospecting: 10-mile radius of 2020 N Monroe, ages 25-55, dining/restaurant/food interests
- Retargeting: Page engagers, site visitors, video viewers (75%+), email list custom audience
- Lookalike: 1% of Uno Mas Rewards: The Cantina Club loyalty list (if uploadable — these guests spend 107% more)
- Exclusions: Recent converters if trackable

**Campaign objective**: Reach (week 1) → Traffic (week 2+)

**Creative format priority**:
1. Static hero dish images (1080x1080) — Surf & Turf, Feast, Carne Asada, Oysters
2. Carousel — "So we did a thing…" → dish-by-dish reveal
3. 15s vertical video — dinner montage (plating, table, reaction)

**Messaging priority**: Lead with the most surprising dish (Surf & Turf or Raw Bar) to break expectations. The "wait, Uno Más has THAT?" moment is the scroll-stopper.

**Bid strategy**: Lowest cost, no caps during learning phase. Monitor CPC — alert above $2.50.

**Creative refresh**: April 14 — swap in new dish photos. If frequency > 3.0 before then, refresh earlier.

---

### Google Search

**Role in campaign**: Capture the search intent that awareness creates
**Budget**: $500 (~$17/day)
**Start date**: March 28

**Audience targeting**:
- Location: 10-mile radius (+25% bid adjustment for 0-5 miles)
- Keywords: Branded ("uno mas dinner") + local intent ("dinner Spokane," "dinner near me")
- Negative keywords: delivery, takeout, fast food, jobs, card game, recipes

**Campaign objective**: Maximize clicks → $2.50 max CPC (local), $1.25 (branded)

**Ad schedule**: Weight toward 11am–8pm (dinner decision hours)

**Messaging priority**: Specific — "Surf & Turf. $47. Worth It." beats "Come try our new menu."

---

### Email (Klaviyo)

**Role in campaign**: Highest-ROI channel — convert existing guests who already know and love Uno Más
**Budget**: $0 (owned)
**Start date**: March 27-28

**Sequence**: Big Reveal → 3 Weekly Dish Spotlights (Fridays — weekend dinner driver timing)

**Segment strategy**:
- Full list for Big Reveal (this is a brand moment, not a promo)
- Uno Mas Rewards: The Cantina Club VIPs get early/exclusive send if segmentable
- Non-openers of Reveal get resend with different subject 3 days later
- Spotlights go to full engaged list

**Key metric**: 35%+ open rate on Reveal. 4%+ click rate.

**Integration note**: Klaviyo is connected to Toast loyalty data. Leverage Uno Mas Rewards: The Cantina Club segments for targeting. After campaign, track if email recipients show up in Toast dinner cover data.

---

### SMS (Klaviyo)

**Role in campaign**: High-urgency action trigger — 3 sends over 30 days
**Budget**: $0 (owned)
**Start date**: March 28

**Send times**: Saturday 4pm (launch), Wednesday 5pm (nudge), Wednesday 4:30pm (final) — all timed to dinner decision windows.

**Key metric**: 15%+ click rate. Alert if unsubscribe rate > 2% on any single send.

**Integration note**: SMS list via Klaviyo/Toast. Keep messages specific (name a dish + price) and short. The Uno Más voice applies — "That's the move" not "Visit us today."

---

### Organic Social (Instagram + Facebook via Vista Social)

**Role in campaign**: Community engine. Social proof loop. The connective tissue between paid and owned.
**Budget**: $0 (time investment)
**Start date**: March 24 (warm-up starts before push)

**Content cadence**:
- Prep week (Mar 24-27): 1 feed post + daily stories (building anticipation)
- Push week 1 (Mar 28–Apr 3): Daily feed posts + stories + 1 Reel
- Sustain (Apr 4–27): 4x/week feed posts + stories. UGC reposts as available.

**Content pillars for this campaign**:
1. **Dish spotlights** — one hero dish per post, specific name + price + one-line story
2. **Soft launch social proof** — "The verdict is in" energy, guest reactions
3. **Unexpected reveals** — "Yeah, we have oysters now 👀"
4. **Table/lifestyle shots** — friends at dinner, the vibe, the energy
5. **UGC reposts** — guest photos/stories shared with permission

**Publishing**: Schedule via Vista Social. Hashtags: #UnoMas #GetALittleLost #SpokaneEats #DinnerHitsDifferent #2020NMonroe

---

### In-Store

**Role**: Upsell dinner plates to guests already in the building. Spark word of mouth.
**Budget**: ~$200 (printing)
**Start date**: March 28

**Tactics**:
- Table cards on every table: Hero dish photo + "Dinner hits different now." + 3 plate callouts with prices
- Staff script: "Have you been in since we dropped the dinner menu? You need to try the [Surf & Turf / Carne Asada / Feast]. It's a whole different experience now."
- Encourage photo sharing: "Tag @unomastacoshop — we repost the best ones"

## Cross-Channel Sequencing

```
[Already Done] → Soft launch validated the menu. Guests love it.
     ↓
[Awareness] → Meta prospecting (hero dish ads) + Organic social blitz + Big Reveal email
     ↓
[Interest] → Google Search captures "uno mas dinner" + "dinner Spokane" searches
     ↓
[Consideration] → Meta retargeting (menu page visitors) + Weekly dish spotlight emails
     ↓
[Decision] → SMS nudge (timed to dinner decision windows) + Retargeting ad
     ↓
[Visit] → In-store table cards + staff talking points → guest tries dinner plates
     ↓
[Advocacy] → Guest posts on Instagram → UGC repost → Social proof loop feeds back to Awareness
```

**Suppression**: If possible, suppress retargeting for users who've visited the menu page 3+ times (they're likely coming — don't over-serve).
