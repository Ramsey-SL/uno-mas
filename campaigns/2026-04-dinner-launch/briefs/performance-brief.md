## Optimization Playbook

**Days 1–3 (Validation)**:
- Confirm all ads delivering. Confirm tracking firing. Check for disapprovals.
- Let all creative variants run — DO NOT optimize yet.

**Days 4–7 (First Read)**:
- Rank variants by CTR. Pause anything below 0.8%.
- Check Google Search terms report — add negatives for irrelevant queries.
- Launch retargeting campaign (Mar 31).

**Week 2 (Optimize)**:
- Consolidate prospecting budget to top 2-3 ad variants.
- Ramp retargeting budget.
- Review email performance — if reveal open rate < 25%, resend to non-openers with new subject.
- A/B test: static vs. video vs. carousel — which format wins?

**Week 3 (Refresh)**:
- Swap in fresh creative (new dish photos) — combat ad fatigue.
- If frequency > 3.0 on any ad set → swap creative or narrow audience.
- Check retargeting pool size — expand to 60-day engagers if too small.

**Week 4 (Consolidate + Close)**:
- Reduce to top 1-2 ads only.
- Shift: 40% prospecting / 60% retargeting (warm audience is most efficient late-campaign).
- Final SMS + email push.
- Wind down budget April 25–27.
