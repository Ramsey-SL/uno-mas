# Differentiation & Competitive Position — Uno Más Tacos & Tequila

## Our Position
- **How we're different:** Three-venue concept in a converted mechanic's garage — a cantina floor, a rooftop speakeasy, and a patio with a full outdoor bar and street food-style outdoor kitchen. Paired with a full dinner program, off-site catering, and a private events operation no competitor in Spokane's Latin segment can match.
- **Why that matters:** Spokane lacks a dining destination that delivers on food quality, atmosphere, and genuine flexibility for any occasion — from a weeknight dinner to a corporate reception to a 200-person buyout. Uno Más covers all of it.
- **Why customers choose us over alternatives:** Because they want to feel like they found something, not just went somewhere. "You *have* to go to Uno Más" is the sentence we're engineering.

## Key Differentiators

### The Three Venues
- **The Cantina** — ground floor, converted mechanic's garage. Inside feels like outside, feels like somewhere else entirely. Full lunch and dinner program, craft cocktails, full bar.
- **The Mezzanine** — upstairs speakeasy and private event space. Moody, atmospheric, private. Leather lounges, fireplace, full bar. Buyouts from intimate dinners to 60-person events.
- **The Patio** — outdoor bar and street food-style outdoor kitchen. A third distinct experience at the same address. Ideal for casual dining, watch parties, large group events, and patio season programming.

### Food & Beverage
- **House-smoked meats** — made in-house; sets flavor apart from anyone using pre-cooked protein
- **Full dinner program** — Carne Asada ($37), Surf & Turf ($47), Achiote Cilantro Shrimp ($30), Raw Bar (Oysters, Ceviche)
- **Uno Más Feast ($129)** — feeds 2–3; nothing like it exists in Spokane's Latin segment
- **Espresso Margarita collab with Indaba Coffee** — uniquely Spokane, not replicable elsewhere
- **Full craft cocktail menu + serious tequila program**

### Events & Private Dining
- **Private events across three venues** — Mezzanine buyouts, patio takeovers, full restaurant buyouts. Work parties, birthdays, receptions, watch parties, rehearsal dinners, corporate gatherings, and more.
- **Off-site catering** — $55K+ in 2025 revenue with no dedicated sales effort; significant growth runway
- **Venue flexibility** — intimate (Mezzanine, 20–60 guests) to large-format (patio + cantina combined)
- **Events contact:** karissa@unomastacoshop.com

### Loyalty & Retention
- **Uno Mas Rewards: The Cantina Club loyalty program** — members spend 107% more; visit 2x as often; 10% value return
- **21+ after 9pm** — intentional atmosphere preservation; not a policy, a design choice
