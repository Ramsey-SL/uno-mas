# Financial Context — Uno Más Tacos & Tequila

*Data source: SBA Loan Narrative, February 2026. High-level summary for marketing context only.*

## Revenue
- **Annual Range:** $500K–$1M (2025 actuals)
- **Primary Streams:**
  - Dine-in / takeout — core business, ~94% of revenue
  - Catering — ~6% of revenue in 2025, growing with no dedicated sales effort to date
  - Mezzanine private events — newly activated Q4 2025; highest-margin channel (no table-turn cost, high beverage attach)

## Growth Goals
- **Target:** ~15% annual revenue growth
- **Key growth levers:**
  - Dinner menu (launched March 2026) — drives higher average ticket and positions as dinner destination
  - Patio season activation — incremental covers and outdoor bar revenue
  - Mezzanine private events — $2,500+ average event, structured sales effort underway
  - Catering — target $8,000/month through structured outreach; significant upside from $55K 2025 baseline

## Marketing Implications
- Dinner menu and private events are the highest-margin growth channels — prioritize in content and paid strategy
- Catering has strong upside with minimal investment — warm outreach and social proof are the unlock
- Average loyalty member spend: $66.99 vs. $32.44 non-loyalty — loyalty acquisition is high-ROI
- Price confidence in copy: never apologize for or justify pricing; the experience sells itself
