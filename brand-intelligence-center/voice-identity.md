# Brand Voice & Identity — Uno Más Tacos & Tequila

## Personality
**We are:** Confident, playful, self-aware, community-driven, just a little chaotic

## Voice Traits
| Trait | What It Means In Practice |
|-------|--------------------------|
| Self-aware | We know we're more than what people expect — we own that with humor, not explanation |
| Confident | We don't hedge. We don't apologize. We say what it is. |
| Group-chat energy | Reads like a text from the friend who always knows where to go |
| Comedic without trying | The humor lands because it's honest, not because it's trying to be funny |
| Peer first | We talk to guests like they're already in on it — never like we're selling to strangers |

## Brand Persona
The friend who starts every story with "so we did a thing…" and somehow always knows where the party is. Jester + Everyman with a dash of Rebel. Confident without being cocky. Honest about the chaos. Deeply invested in you having a good time — and a great meal.

**Comedian energy reference:** Tom Segura + Bert Kreischer. Tom Segura is the default register — dry, precise, earns the laugh; use for dinner, The Mezzanine, and brand-level content. Bert Kreischer is the lunch/casual/social register — high energy, self-aware, story-driven, zero filter but never mean.

## Tone Range by Context
| Context | Tone | Emojis | Notes |
|---------|------|--------|-------|
| Lunch / casual content | Casual, energetic, playful | 3–5 | Full personality. Closest to original voice. |
| Dinner / elevated plates | Confident, considered, quiet | 1–2 max | Let the food speak. Always include reservation CTA. |
| Cocktails / happy hour | Flirty, self-aware | 2–3 | Lead with the drink, anchor to the experience |
| Patio / outdoor | Casual, social, inviting | 3–5 | Energy of an afternoon that turns into a night |
| The Mezzanine | Cool, minimal, moody | 0–1 | Separate sub-brand voice — see below |
| Paid ads | Hook-driven, clarity first | 1–2 | 60% clever max — clarity over cleverness |
| Email / SMS | Friend texting good news | 1–3 | Personal, always include logistics |
| Social organic | Full personality | 3–5 | Most expressive channel — let it breathe |
| Review responses | Warm, direct, never corporate | 0 | Under 100 words, sign as "The Uno Más Team" |
| Private events / catering | Confident and easy-breezy | 1–2 | Make it sound like zero work for them |

## ALWAYS
- Always feel like it came from a real person, not a corporation
- Always reflect Spokane pride and local community identity
- Always lead with the experience, prove it with the food
- Always sound like you're in on the joke
- Always give people something to share, quote, or remember
- Always be honest — about the chaos, the price, the vibe
- Short sentences. Fragments welcome. Parenthetical asides.
- For dinner copy: allow a slightly more elevated tone — still Uno Más, but grown up for the occasion
- Price confidence: never apologize, explain, or justify what things cost

## NEVER
- Never corporate. Never apologetic.
- Never use: taco shop (in brand descriptions) ❌
- Never use: authentic Mexican ❌
- Never use: street tacos (in brand-level copy — fine in direct menu references) ❌
- Never use: mouthwatering ❌
- Never use: culinary journey ❌
- Never use: leverage / utilize ❌
- Never use: artisanal ❌
- Never use: mixology ❌
- Never use: amazing (generic) ❌
- Never use: "We apologize for…" ❌
- Never use: vibrant (overused hospitality filler) ❌
- Never use: "perfect for any occasion" ❌
- Never stack adjectives: "fresh, delicious, flavorful, hearty" ❌
- Never try too hard to sound cool — just be cool

## The Mezzanine — Sub-Brand Voice

The Mezzanine is the cool older sibling. It shares Uno Más DNA but has its own distinct register. More polished. More atmosphere-forward. Still warm — never stiff.

**Tone:** Dark, minimal, intentional. Lead with atmosphere over information.
**Humor dial:** 2–3/10 — precise and well-timed, not constant
**Emojis:** 0–1 per post

**What it sounds like:**
- "Upstairs, it gets quieter. Better."
- "What happens when a speakeasy and a rooftop bar decide to share a space."
- "Private. Moody. The kind of room where whatever you're celebrating actually feels celebrated."
- "An unforgettable evening — with tacos and tequila."

**What it never does:** Generic event venue language. "Perfect for any occasion." Forced warmth. Uno Más casual energy.

## Visual Identity — Uno Más

### Color Palette
| Name | Hex | Use For |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Pink** | `#E22690` | Primary brand color — logo, key highlights, hero moments |
| **Blue** | `#18BCDC` | Headlines, accents, CTAs |
| **Navy** | `#003366` | Dark backgrounds, text on light |
| **Yellow** | `#FFEC00` | Accents, callouts, energy moments |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, reversed text |
| **Black** | `#000000` | Body text, dark UI |

**Note:** Pink is the hero color — don't let Navy or Blue dominate. Pink is the primary brand color in the logo and key highlights.

### Typography
| Role | Font | Use For |
|------|------|---------|
| **Headlines / Display** | Antonio (Bold, Regular) | All primary headlines, display text, menu category titles |
| **Body / UI** | Montserrat (Light, Regular, Medium, Bold) | Body copy, captions, UI labels, descriptions |

### Logo
- **Primary:** "Uno Más" script + agave icon + "TACOS & TEQUILA" bar
- **Icon:** Pink agave plant — standalone for icon/watermark/favicon use
- **Primary color:** Full color (Pink on White) — default
- **Reversed:** White on dark/Navy background
- **Canva Brand Kit ID:** `kAFqKpAzOh0`

### Aesthetic
Converted mechanic's garage meets Latin cantina. Inside feels like outside — a courtyard off a side street in Mexico. Raw textures, warm atmospheric lighting, neon accents. The room does most of the work. Dinner and Mezzanine content leans darker, more atmospheric — still unmistakably Uno Más.

## Visual Identity — The Mezzanine

### Color Palette
| Name | Hex | Use For |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Electric Pink** | `#E22790` | Primary brand color — bold, electric |
| **Magenta** | `#BF28BF` | Secondary accent |
| **Ultra Violet** | `#93009B` | Deep accent, dark UI |
| **Charcoal** | `#333333` | Dark backgrounds, text |
| **Deep Black** | `#000000` | Primary dark background |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Reversed text, light elements |

**Note:** Black and Charcoal are the foundation. Electric Pink through Ultra Violet create intensity. This is a nightlife palette — not food-forward. Electric Pink is the only warm element — use it intentionally.

### Typography
| Role | Font | Use For |
|------|------|---------|
| **Titles / Display** | DIN Condensed VF (Demi Bold, Regular, Light) | All headlines, event titles, big announcements |
| **Body / UI** | Poppins (Black, Bold, Regular, Medium, Light, Thin) | Body copy, descriptions, labels |
| **Accent Callouts** | Baka Too (Regular) | Decorative callouts, signature moments |

### Logo
- **Primary:** "UM" tribal/geometric mark + "MEZZANINE" wordmark
- **Primary use:** White on Black (default)
- **Bold use:** Electric Pink on Black
- **Premium use:** Gradient (Pink → Magenta → Ultra Violet) on Black
- **Canva Brand Kit ID:** `kAGze1MPDmA`

### Aesthetic
Dark, electric, and dramatic. Leather lounges. Fireplace. Upstairs, it gets quieter. Better. Watercolor backgrounds are an approved Mezzanine design element.

## Brand Separation Rules
| Content Type | Use Uno Más Brand | Use Mezzanine Brand |
|---|---|---|
| Food, cocktails, main floor | ✅ | ❌ |
| Patio content | ✅ | ❌ |
| Sunday Brunch | ✅ | ❌ |
| General community / restaurant content | ✅ | ❌ |
| Mezzanine atmosphere / late night | ❌ | ✅ |
| Private events in The Mezzanine | ❌ | ✅ |
| Mezzanine event packages | ❌ | ✅ |

**Never mix:** Uno Más Pink (`#E22690`) with Mezzanine typography in the same design. Never use Antonio in Mezzanine designs. Never use DIN Condensed in Uno Más designs.
