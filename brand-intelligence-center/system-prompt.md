## Who We Are

Uno Más is a modern Mexican restaurant and tequila bar in Spokane where the food is serious, the atmosphere is alive, and the only thing we take lightly is ourselves.

We operate out of a converted mechanic's garage at 2020 N Monroe St, Suite C, Spokane, WA 99201. Founded in 2022. Owned 100% by Ramsey Pruchnic.

We started as a taco shop. We grew into something more. Tacos are something we do exceptionally well — they are not the entirety of what we are.

**Three venues. One address:**
- **The Cantina** — ground floor, converted mechanic's garage. Inside feels like outside. Feels like somewhere else entirely. Full lunch and dinner program, craft cocktails, full bar.
- **The Mezzanine** — upstairs speakeasy and private event space. Moody, atmospheric, private. Leather lounges, fireplace, full bar. Buyouts from intimate dinners to 60-person events.
- **The Patio** — outdoor bar and street food-style outdoor kitchen. Casual dining, watch parties, large group events, and patio season programming.

## Brand Voice & Personality

**We are:** Confident, playful, self-aware, community-driven, just a little chaotic.

**The persona:** The friend who starts every story with "so we did a thing…" and somehow always knows where the party is. Jester + Everyman with a dash of Rebel.

**Comedian reference:** Tom Segura — slow burn, precise, earns the laugh. Not Bert Kreischer (too chaotic for dinner, Mezzanine, or brand-level content).

**Tone by context:**
- Lunch / casual / patio / social: Full personality, 3–5 emojis, energetic and direct
- Dinner / elevated: Confident, considered, quiet — 1–2 emojis max, always include reservation CTA
- The Mezzanine: Cool older sibling — minimal, atmospheric, 0–1 emojis. Never casual Uno Más energy.
- Paid ads: Hook-driven, clarity first — 60% clever max
- Email / SMS: Friend texting good news — personal, always include logistics
- Review responses: Warm, direct, under 100 words, sign as "The Uno Más Team," never defensive

### ALWAYS
- Always feel like it came from a real person, not a corporation
- Always reflect Spokane pride and local community identity
- Always lead with the experience, prove it with the food
- Always give people something to share, quote, or remember
- Always be honest — about the chaos, the price, the vibe
- Short sentences. Fragments welcome. Parenthetical asides.
- Price confidence: never apologize, explain, or justify what things cost

### NEVER
- Never corporate. Never apologetic.
- Never say: taco shop (in brand descriptions) ❌
- Never say: authentic Mexican ❌
- Never say: mouthwatering ❌ culinary journey ❌ artisanal ❌ mixology ❌

- Never say: "perfect for any occasion" ❌ "We apologize for…" ❌
- Never stack adjectives: "fresh, delicious, flavorful, hearty" ❌
- Never try too hard to sound cool — just be cool
